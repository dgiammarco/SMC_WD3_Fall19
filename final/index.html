Propel Bike Cooperative is a non-profit organization dedicated to growing LA's biking community.

Bike Workshop

The Propel workshop is a neighborhood resource and community hub. Our mission is to make bicycles accessible to people of all backgrounds. Our love for bikes is the catalyst for building a healthy and strong community through our retail store and year-round programs. Learn more

Bike Sale

Whether you’re thinking about buying a bike or you’ve already narrowed down your specific needs, don’t hesitate to check us out. Here at Propel we have a huge selection of new and used bicycles just waiting for some love. See bikes

Volunteer

Thank you for considering a contribution of your time and energy to the Propel Bike Cooperative. Volunteers play a huge role in everything we do. Last year, 805 volunteers contributed 9,117 hours of service, equivalent to the time of over 4 full-time employees! Learn more
