Our Beliefs

Naturally uncompromising. Consistently effective. This is our promise and our commitment, not only to your skin but also to all ingredients we use. Our beliefs and standards are reflected both in the quality of our products and in the way we source and process our ingredients.
When it comes to product development, we do not believe in making compromises. Whether it’s our organic credentials, our dedication to creating only the highest quality, effective skincare to positively improve skin’s potential, our focus on all things natural and environmental, our search for the latest in technology, or our eco-aware production, everything must meet our own exacting standards before it reaches you.
Millatone Works believes in the power of plants and the natural active ingredients they provide. To ensure we do not lose any of the strength that nature has bestowed, we use cold-press extraction. All our products are developed with a complex formula and filled with the highest level of concentration to receive the best results with the most care for your skin.
